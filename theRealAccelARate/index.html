Our Purpose

With the use of augmented reality, we wish to help students become more interested and engaged in their physics classes. Try our program by clicking above!

Calling All Athletes

Although AccelARate is for all students, it is especially helpful for those who are more athletically inclined. Just imagine the white sphere as a basketball, baseball, or any of your liking and learn!
